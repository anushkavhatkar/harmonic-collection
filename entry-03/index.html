love letters to colour love

entry 03: drama

Colours are beautiful. Some of the most brilliant colours throughout history have been some of the deadliest. Here’s some of them.

In 1898, Marie and Pierre Curie discovered radium. Claimed to have restorative properties, it was added to toothpaste, medicine, water, and food. It was the most luminous, glowing green. We started using it in beauty products and cosmetics. It wasn’t until the mid-20th century that we realized radium’s harmful effects as a radioactive element outweighed its remarkable visual benefits.
