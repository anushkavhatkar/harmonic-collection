The first thing that broke my mind when it came to understanding color is thinking of them in relativity, as cool or warm toned. In general, colors leaning towards red or having red undertones can be considered warm. Colors leaning green or having green undertones are cooler.

Producing and mixing secondary colors, then, came down to understanding whether the color is inherently cool or warm. And then using the corresponding primary colors.
